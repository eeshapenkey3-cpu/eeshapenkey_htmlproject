ABOUT US

We're a team of four first-year college students passionate about tackling the dual challenges of climate change and energy poverty. This project, born from our collective interest in affordable and clean energy, is dedicated to exploring and promoting sustainable energy solutions. We believe that everyone deserves access to energy that is both environmentally responsible and economically viable. Join us as we learn, share, and advocate for a future powered by clean, accessible energy for all.

Our Goal

Our primary goal is to contribute actively to the realization of UN Sustainable Development Goal 7 (SDG 7): Affordable and Clean Energy. We aim to achieve this by serving as a dynamic, student-led resource hub that educates and engages our peers and the wider community. We want this platform to successfully demystify complex clean energy topics, such as renewable technology and sustainable policy, making them accessible and understandable to everyone. Simultaneously, we are focused on highlighting affordable solutions by showcasing innovative, low-cost energy models suitable for diverse settings. Ultimately, we seek to foster constructive dialogue and collaboration on the necessity of a just and clean energy transition. We want this project to be a stepping stone in our academic careers, translating our classroom learning into real-world advocacy and impact.
